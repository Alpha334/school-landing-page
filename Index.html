About Detlof Prep School

Detlof Preparatory School is a joyful space where children grow with confidence, curiosity, and creativity. We provide a solid academic foundation wrapped in fun learning experiences, playful discovery, and genuine care. Our loving teachers and vibrant classrooms make school feel like a second home.
