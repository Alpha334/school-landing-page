Excursion to Kumasi Zoo

Our Grade 4 pupils had an unforgettable day at the Kumasi Zoo. They learned about animal habitats, biodiversity, and conservation efforts. The trip also included a fun lunch picnic and quiz session. Special thanks to our amazing teachers and volunteers!

Spelling Bee Finalist!

Sarah Asante from Class 6A represented the school in the Ashanti Regional Spelling Bee competition and made it to the finals! Her success brings pride and inspiration to all our students. Keep aiming high, Sarah!

Cultural Day

Our annual Cultural Day was a vibrant display of Ghana's rich traditions. Students performed dances, displayed crafts, and shared food from different regions. It was a joyful reminder of the beauty in diversity. Thank you to all parents who contributed!
